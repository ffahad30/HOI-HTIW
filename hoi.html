Oct. 1957

Russia puts Sputnik into orbit

NASA opens for business via Eisenhower executive order

July 1961

Leonard Kleinrock of MIT wrote the first paper on packet switching theory

Aug. 1962

J.C.R. Licklider of MIT - memos on “Galactic Network Concept”

Oct. 1969

Larry Roberts heads project that connects first two ARPANET sites

Oct. 1972

Bob Kahn demonstrates ARPANET at the ICCC

March 1972

Ray Tomlinson writes first basic email program using “@”

May 1974

Vinton Cerf & Ray Kahn produce A Protocol for Packet Network Interconnection

1978

TCP and IP split

1980

Tim Berners-Lee - writes predecessor to WWW

Nov. 1982

Jon Postel, Paul Mockapedia, and Craig Partridge design DNS

1990

Tim Berners-Lee creates World Wide Web

1993

Marc Andreesen develops Mosaic web browser
